About Speculum

Digital preservation through local mirroring

The Project

Speculum is a digital archiving initiative dedicated to preserving online content at risk of disappearing. We mirror websites, YouTube channels, and digital publications for future generations.

The name "Speculum" (mirror in Latin) reflects our mission: creating faithful reflections of the web before it vanishes. Every site we archive becomes a permanent record of digital culture.

What We Archive

  • Personal websites and blogs
  • Independent publications and zines
  • YouTube channels and video content
  • Community forums and archives
  • Cultural documentation
  • Sites at risk of closure

Technology

Speculum uses open source tools for archiving:

  • wget — Complete website mirroring
  • yt-dlp — YouTube video archiving
  • SingleFile — JavaScript-heavy sites
  • Ollama — AI-powered categorization

Contribute

You can help preserve the web:

  • Submit sites at risk through our request form
  • Spread the word in your community
