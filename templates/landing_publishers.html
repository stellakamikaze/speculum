03.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Speculum free for independent publishers?
Yes, Speculum is completely free. We archive websites and YouTube channels at no cost to creators. Our mission is preservation, not profit.
What happens to my content if my hosting expires?
Once archived in Speculum, your content remains permanently accessible even if your original hosting expires, your domain lapses, or your platform shuts down. We create complete, navigable mirrors.
Can I request my own website to be archived?
Absolutely. We encourage creators to submit their own content for preservation. Use our request form to submit your website or YouTube channel URL, and we'll archive it for posterity.
How do I know my content has been successfully archived?
After archiving, you'll find your site in our catalog with a status indicator. You can browse the archived version to verify completeness. We also integrate with the Internet Archive's Wayback Machine for additional redundancy.
