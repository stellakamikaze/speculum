Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Data Collection

Speculum collects minimal data necessary to operate the archive service. When you submit a mirror request, we store:

  • The URL you submitted
  • Optional name and contact email
  • Reason for the request
  • Submission timestamp

Navigation Data

Our systems automatically collect:

  • IP address (anonymized after 7 days)
  • Date and time of request
  • Pages visited
  • Browser and operating system

Purpose

Data is used for:

  • Processing archive requests
  • Technical operation of the service
  • Abuse prevention and security
  • Anonymous aggregate statistics

Cookies

We use only essential technical cookies for session management and language preferences. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Archived Content

The websites we archive are publicly accessible. We do not modify the content we preserve. If you are the owner of an archived site and wish to have it removed, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing

To exercise these rights, visit our contact page.

Security

We implement technical measures to protect data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Secure password hashing (scrypt)
  • CSRF protection on all forms
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
