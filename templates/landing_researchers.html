03.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cite content from Speculum in academic papers?
Each archived page has a permanent URL and timestamp. Use the archive URL with the access date for citations. We provide structured metadata including original URL, archive date, and content description for proper academic attribution.
Can I search the full text of archived websites?
Yes, Speculum includes full-text search powered by SQLite FTS5. You can search across all archived content, filter by category, date range, and content type to find relevant sources for your research.
What types of content are preserved in Speculum?
Speculum preserves websites, blogs, YouTube channels, independent publications, community forums, and cultural documentation. We focus on at-risk content including counterculture, underground scenes, and independent media.
Is Speculum content accessible for research purposes?
Yes, all archived content is freely accessible for research, education, and preservation purposes. We provide RSS feeds, API access, and structured metadata to facilitate academic use.
